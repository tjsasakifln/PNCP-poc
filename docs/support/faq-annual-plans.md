### Can I switch from monthly to annual mid-cycle?

**Answer:** Yes! You can upgrade from monthly to annual anytime.

**How It Works:**
1. You'll receive a **pro-rated credit** for the unused portion of your current monthly period
2. Your annual subscription starts immediately
3. You're charged the annual amount minus the credit

**Example:**
- Current plan: Consultor √Ågil Monthly (R$ 297/month)
- 15 days remaining in your monthly period
- Unused credit: R$ 148.50 (15/30 √ó R$ 297)
- You pay: R$ 2,851 - R$ 148.50 = **R$ 2,702.50** upfront
- Next charge: Feb 7, 2027 (12 months from upgrade)

**No Penalties:** You can upgrade anytime without losing money.

### What happens at renewal time?

**Answer:** Your annual subscription automatically renews unless you cancel before the renewal date.

**Renewal Process:**
1. **7 days before renewal:** We'll send a reminder email with the renewal amount
2. **Renewal date:** Your payment method on file is charged automatically
3. **After renewal:** You receive a receipt and your subscription continues for another year

**To Avoid Renewal:** Cancel at least 24 hours before your renewal date. You'll retain access until the end of your paid period.

## Refunds & Cancellations

### Can I get a refund?

**Answer:** It depends on when you purchased your annual plan.

| **0-7 days** | ‚úÖ **Full refund (100%)** - No questions asked (Brazilian CDC compliance) |

### What if I want to cancel after 7 days?

**Answer:** You won't receive a monetary refund, but you'll retain all your annual plan benefits until the end of your subscription period. After that, you can choose to:
1. **Convert to monthly billing** (automatic, no action needed)
2. **Cancel completely** and stop all charges

**Example:**
- Purchased annual plan on **Jan 1, 2026**
- Request cancellation on **March 1, 2026** (60 days later)
- **No refund**, but you keep:
  - Early access to new features
  - Proactive search (when it launches in March 2026)
  - AI edital analysis (Sala de Guerra only, when it launches in April 2026)
  - All core plan features
- **Subscription expires:** Dec 31, 2026
- **Jan 1, 2027:** Either convert to monthly or subscription ends (you choose)

---

### Are there any exceptions for refunds after 7 days?

**Answer:** Yes. You can receive a full refund anytime if:

1. **Service Downtime:** Service is unavailable for more than 72 consecutive hours
2. **Feature Delay:** A promised annual-exclusive feature is delayed more than 6 months past the stated date
3. **Unauthorized Charge:** Fraudulent charge or billing error

**Example (Feature Delay):**
- You purchased annual plan in February 2026 for early access to "Proactive Search" (promised March 2026)
- Feature is delayed to October 2026 (7 months late)
- You qualify for full refund regardless of when you purchased

**To Request Exception Refund:** Contact suporte@smartlic.tech with details of the issue.

---

### Will I lose my data if I cancel?

**Answer:** No. Your data is retained for **30 days** after cancellation to allow you to:
- Download your saved searches
- Export search history
- Resubscribe without losing data

**What Happens to Data:**

**To Export Data:** Go to **Account Settings** ‚Üí **Export Data** (available anytime during or after subscription).

### Can I change my plan tier (e.g., Consultor ‚Üí M√°quina) while on annual billing?

**How It Works:**
