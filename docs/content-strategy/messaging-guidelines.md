### Rule 3: Document Methodology for Legal Safety

**For Each Quantitative Claim, Document:**

- Assumptions: Manual search includes: keyword brainstorming (20 min), portal navigation (30 min), search execution (2h), result filtering (4h), document reading (2h) = ~8.5h avg

## âœ… Content Review Checklist

Before publishing any user-facing content, validate:

### 1. Strategic Alignment
- [ ] Positions SmartLic as "intelligence tool", not "PNCP aggregator"
- [ ] Emphasizes unfair advantages: Speed, Precision, Consolidation, AI

- [ ] Clear next action for user
- [ ] Sense of urgency or value gain
